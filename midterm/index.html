Vibes LA is a comprehensive fitness center that provides yoga classes, including flow yoga, hatha yoga, shoulder and neck physiotherapy yoga, aerial yoga, and maternity yoga. Dance courses include Kpop, Hiphop, Jazzfunk, Heels, pole dancing and children's street dancing. Fitness courses include aerobic fighting, physical core training, Zumba dance, and intensive training courses. Whether you like static and dynamic, beginner or advanced, you can find a course that suits you here.
