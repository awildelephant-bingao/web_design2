Heart of Los Angeles

Heart of Los Angeles (HOLA) gives underserved kids an equal chance to succeed through a comprehensive array of after-school academic, arts, athletics and wellness programs. Our success is founded on safe environments, nurtured by no-wrong-door approach, amplified by world-renowned partners, and perpetuated by students who become productive, caring, and responsible citizens. All of HOLA’s programs continue to be offered at no cost to the families who we serve.

YOU CAN HELP STUDENTS AND FAMILIES AFFECTED BY COVID-19

Throughout our 31-year history, the commitment of Heart of Los Angeles (HOLA) has been to give the youth we serve everything they need to achieve their full potential, and the advent of COVID-19 has not changed our focus on that goal. We have been adapting our services to a remote model that will ensure the disruptions in our students’ education, economic stability, and mental wellbeing are addressed before they can derail an already challenging journey to achieving full potential and overcoming poverty.
